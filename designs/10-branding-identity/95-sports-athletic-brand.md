# Design 95: Sports & Athletic Brand

**Category:** Branding & Identity  
**Style Tags:** sports, athletic, Nike, kinetic, bold, performance  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Best For:** Sports brand presentations, athletic product launches, sports organization strategy sessions, fitness brand pitches, and any context where communicating energy, performance, and competitive excellence is the goal. Ideal for sports marketers, athletic brand managers, and sports organizations.

---

## Visual Direction

Nike, Adidas, and Under Armour brand communication aesthetics — bold typography at extreme scale, action photography, kinetic diagonal compositions, performance-metric callouts, team photography, and the visual vocabulary of winning, effort, and athletic excellence. The design makes you want to move.

## Background & Texture

Deep black (#0A0A0A) — the classic sports brand presentation background that makes everything pop. Bold color zones in brand color. Dynamic image backgrounds with high-energy action photography. Carbon fiber or performance material texture at very low opacity as an optional surface element.

## Typography

Bold, compressed, impact-weight sans-serif — the font category of sports communication. Impact, Bebas Neue, League Gothic, or similar. Type at extreme scale (headline running edge to edge). Numbers for statistics in the same bold weight at very large scale. Italicized type for speed/motion. All-caps as the default.

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** Matte black (#0A0A0A) — the dominant presence
- **Secondary:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) — primary type on dark backgrounds
- **Accent 1:** Performance color (Nike orange #E87722, Adidas vivid yellow, Under Armour red) — the brand energy color
- **Accent 2:** Electric blue or neon accent (#00E5FF) — technology and performance science
- **Neutral:** Mid gray (#4A4A4A) — secondary information

## Layout & Composition

Diagonal dynamic composition — everything at an angle or in motion. Full-bleed action photography as background. Typography bleeding off the slide edges at extreme scale. A bold diagonal color zone dividing the composition. The single most important statistic or word taking 60% of the slide in maximum bold type.

## Graphic Elements & Decorations

Bold performance metrics in large type (ZERO LIMITS, 10X FASTER, 100% COMMITMENT). Action photography with motion blur and dramatic lighting. Nike-style single athlete at maximum scale. Diagonal rule lines creating energy. Brand mark at maximum presence. Speed lines suggesting motion. Stadium photography for organizational content.

## Slide Types & Templates

- **Title Slide:** Full-bleed action photography, huge bold white headline across the image at maximum scale, brand mark and collection name in the corner.
- **Content Slide:** Bold statistic or performance claim at extreme scale, supporting context in condensed smaller type, action photography background.
- **Process/Flow Slide:** Training progression or product development arc, stages as bold numbered milestones, energy builds through the sequence.
- **Data/Chart Slide:** Performance metrics as oversized bold numbers with brief labels, comparison data in a simple clear chart with brand accent color.
- **Closing Slide:** Single bold call to action — "JUST DO IT" level declarative — at maximum scale, dark background, no other elements.

## Tone & Mood

Kinetically energetic, performance-aspirational, and competitively confident. The tone communicates: this brand, product, or team is about winning, and winning requires everything you have. There is no comfort zone here — only the next level. Audiences feel energized and motivated to perform.

## Avoid

- Soft or passive visual language
- Light backgrounds (sports brands live in bold darkness)
- Small or restrained typography
- Multiple competing visual elements at similar scale
- Non-action photography
- Any element that suggests rest, comfort, or settling

## Example Prompt

> Design a presentation slide in Nike and athletic sports brand style. Use a deep matte black background, a bold performance statistic in huge Bebas Neue or Impact all-caps white type ("40% FASTER" or "ZERO COMPROMISE") at 80% of the slide height, an action photography placeholder behind the type with dramatic lighting and motion blur, a diagonal brand-color accent zone (orange #E87722) cutting across the lower-left corner, the brand mark in small white in one corner, and supporting performance context in condensed smaller bold type. The feel should be kinetically energetic, performance-aspirational, and boldly competitive — athletic brand aesthetics at maximum intensity.
