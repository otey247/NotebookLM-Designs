# Design 79: Podcast & Audio Brand

**Category:** Storytelling & Narrative  
**Style Tags:** podcast, audio-brand, Spotify, cover-art, audio-visualization, conversational  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Best For:** Podcast program presentations, audio content pitches, radio and audio media industry decks, and any presentation that exists in relationship to audio content production. Ideal for audio content creators, media companies, and anyone presenting podcast strategy.

---

## Visual Direction

Spotify podcast cover art aesthetics, audiogram visual designs, sound wave visualizations, interview-context photography, microphone and studio aesthetics — the visual language of the audio-first media world. The design communicates: the content lives primarily in your ears, and the visual exists to invite you in.

## Background & Texture

Vivid solid-color backgrounds in podcast-cover style: deep purple (#3D0080), electric teal (#006B6B), warm orange (#E87722), or deep midnight blue (#0A0A40). Clean, bold, photographic — the aesthetic of Spotify's visual design system applied to slide backgrounds.

## Typography

Bold, modern sans-serif — the podcast cover art tradition uses maximum typographic weight for maximum visibility at small thumbnail size. Podcast name in massive bold type. Episode title in condensed medium weight. Guest name in light or regular weight. All at high contrast against the vivid background.

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** Deep purple (#3D0080) — Spotify podcast aesthetic primary
- **Secondary:** Electric teal (#006B6B) — audio-visual presence
- **Accent 1:** Warm orange (#E87722) — audio-energy, warmth
- **Accent 2:** Bright yellow-green (#AAFF00) — playback indicator, highlight
- **Neutral:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) — primary text on dark podcast covers

## Layout & Composition

Podcast cover art format adapted to landscape slide: large bold podcast name, host photograph or brand illustration taking 40–50% of the slide, high-contrast typography at large scale. Audio visualization element (waveform, equalizer bars, sound wave circles) as a decorative accent. Microphone icon as a recognizable brand anchor. Spotify green play button as CTA element.

## Graphic Elements & Decorations

Audio waveform visualization (the characteristic waveform of a podcast recording). Circular "audiogram" visualization (sound waves radiating from a center point). Microphone icon in the brand style. Headphone icon. Playback control buttons (play, pause, skip). "Now Playing" or "New Episode" banner. Episode number indicator. Platform icons (Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts).

## Slide Types & Templates

- **Title Slide:** Podcast cover art format — vivid solid background, large bold podcast name, host photography, circular audio visualization, episode title below.
- **Content Slide:** Episode content summary — episode number and title in large bold, guest name, key topics as bold bullet items against the vivid background.
- **Process/Flow Slide:** Podcast series arc — each episode in the series as a "cover art" tile, reading left-to-right as a complete narrative arc.
- **Data/Chart Slide:** Podcast performance metrics (downloads, listeners, ratings) in vivid branded colors with bold statistics, waveform accent.
- **Closing Slide:** "Subscribe and Follow" podcast CTA slide — platform buttons, bold final call to action, brand colors.

## Tone & Mood

Conversational, accessible, and audio-intimate. The tone communicates: this is a human conversation, and you're invited to listen in. There is the specific warmth of the audio medium — a voice in your ear — translated into visual design. Audiences feel the intimacy of the podcast listening experience.

## Avoid

- Corporate or formal design aesthetics
- Small typography (podcast covers are designed for thumbnail sizes)
- Quiet or muted color palettes
- Photography-light designs (most podcast covers are photography-forward)
- Multiple accent colors beyond 2–3
- Any element that makes the design feel formal or institutional

## Example Prompt

> Design a presentation slide in podcast brand and cover art style. Use a vivid deep purple background (#3D0080), bold white podcast name in massive sans-serif type, a host portrait photograph taking the right 40% of the slide, an audio waveform visualization element in bright yellow-green across the lower zone, the episode title in condensed medium-weight type below the podcast name, a warm orange "New Episode" banner in the upper corner, and platform icons (Spotify, Apple Podcasts) in a row at the bottom. The feel should be conversational, audio-intimate, and cover-art vivid — podcast brand aesthetics applied to presentation design.
